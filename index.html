About Us

Professional Notary Services
You Can Count On

Emerald Notary Services, LLC is a full-service notary firm based in Emporium, Pennsylvania, proudly serving Cameron County and surrounding communities. We combine local, in-person service with cutting-edge Remote Online Notary technology — so no matter where you are, we can help.

Whether you need a document notarized at your home, office, hospital, or anywhere in the world via a secure video session, our experienced notary is ready to assist with professionalism and discretion.

  • PA-commissioned Notary Public & Notary Signing Agent
  • Remote Online Notary (RON) available 24/7 worldwide
  • Mobile service — we travel to you
  • Flexible scheduling including evenings & weekends
