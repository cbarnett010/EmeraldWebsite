Pennsylvania State Commission

PA Notary Public

A Pennsylvania Notary Public is a state-commissioned official authorized to verify identities, witness signatures, administer oaths, and perform acknowledgments. Notary services help prevent fraud and ensure that important legal documents are properly executed in compliance with Pennsylvania law.

At Emerald Notary Services LLC, we provide professional and reliable PA notary services for individuals, businesses, and real estate professionals.

Common Documents We Notarize

  • Loan documents
  • Real estate deeds
  • Powers of attorney
  • Wills and trusts
  • Affidavits
  • Contracts and financial documents

Whether you need a single signature notarized or multiple documents completed, we provide accurate and efficient service.

Remote Online Notary (RON)

Our Remote Online Notary (RON) services in Pennsylvania allow documents to be notarized securely through live video conferencing.

As a commissioned Pennsylvania Online Notary, we can perform legally binding electronic notarizations for U.S. citizens located anywhere in the United States or abroad, provided identity verification requirements are met.

What RON Includes

  • Secure identity verification
  • Live video notarization session
  • Electronic signature and seal
  • Digital delivery of completed documents

This service is ideal for out-of-state clients, busy professionals, military members, and U.S. citizens traveling internationally.

Appointments available 24/7 by scheduling in advance. Please visit our Fees page for pricing.

PennDOT Card Agent Services

A PennDOT Card Service Agent assists individuals with various motor vehicle and driver-related services. They are authorized to process transactions such as vehicle registrations and title transfers.

These agents act as intermediaries between the public and the Pennsylvania Department of Transportation (PennDOT), ensuring efficient and accurate processing of essential documents.
