About Our Mobile Service

The Notary That Comes to You

If you are unable to visit our office for any of our PA Public Notary Services, we are pleased to offer mobile services directly to any location of your choosing. We will travel to you within a 50-mile radius of our office in Emporium, PA.

You receive the same professional Pennsylvania notary services at your location of choice — with no compromise in accuracy or professionalism.

Please allow 24 hours for us to accommodate your appointment within our schedule. A transportation fee will be applied based on the distance traveled — visit our Fees page for details.
